4. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15–22)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15) — Request a copy of all data we hold about you via GET /api/gdpr/export
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17) — Request deletion of your data via DELETE /api/gdpr/erase
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16) — Update your profile information through Google account settings
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20) — Export your data in JSON format via the export endpoint
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18) — Contact us to restrict data processing
  • Right to Object (Art. 21) — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw cookie/analytics consent at any time via the cookie settings

To exercise any right, use the in-app data controls or email privacy@huddle-meet.app. We will respond within 30 days.

5. Data Processing & Storage

5.1 EU Data Residency

Huddle supports EU data residency options. When deployed in EU regions (e.g., Render EU, AWS eu-west-1), all meeting data, chat messages, and audit logs remain within the EU. The current preview instance is hosted on Render (US-Oregon). Enterprise deployments can be configured for EU-only residency.

5.2 Encryption

  • In Transit: All connections use TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS/WSS). WebRTC media is encrypted with DTLS-SRTP.
  • At Rest: Database records are encrypted at rest via the hosting provider’s volume encryption (AES-256). Session tokens use HMAC-SHA256.

7. Data Retention & Automatic Cleanup

  • Chat messages are purged 24 hours after meeting ends
  • Shared files are purged 7 days after meeting ends
  • Audit logs are retained for 1 year (ISO 27001 A.12.4.1)
  • Authentication sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity
  • Inactive rooms are cleaned up after 4 hours

8. Security Measures (ISO 27001:2022)

Huddle’s information security management system aligns with ISO 27001:2022 controls:

  • A.5 – Access Control: PIN-based access gating, Firebase authentication, role-based host/participant permissions
  • A.8 – Asset Management: Automated data classification and retention policies for all data categories
  • A.8.10 – Cryptography: HMAC-SHA256 session tokens, DTLS-SRTP media encryption, TLS 1.2+ transport
  • A.8.15 – Logging: Comprehensive audit logging of all security-relevant events with tamper-evident timestamps
  • A.8.16 – Monitoring: Real-time session monitoring, automatic cleanup of expired sessions
  • A.5.23 – Cloud Security: EU data residency option, encrypted storage, sub-processor DPAs
  • A.8.9 – Configuration Management: Environment-based configuration, no hardcoded credentials
  • A.5.24 – Incident Management: Audit trail for breach investigation, 72-hour notification SLA

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will:

  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours (GDPR Art. 33)
  • Notify affected individuals without undue delay if the breach poses a high risk (GDPR Art. 34)
  • Document the breach in the audit log per ISO 27001 A.5.24

10. International Transfers

If data is processed outside the EU/EEA, we ensure adequate protection through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where applicable.

11. Children’s Privacy

Huddle is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted promptly.

12. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Enterprise customers can request a Data Processing Agreement covering:

  • Scope and purpose of processing
  • Categories of personal data and data subjects
  • Technical and organisational security measures
  • Sub-processor management and notification
  • Data breach notification procedures
  • Audit rights and compliance verification
  • Data return and deletion upon termination

Contact privacy@huddle-meet.app for DPA requests.
